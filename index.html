You Found a Hobart!

Hobart is a semi-eternal biological mineral form believed to have originally been constructed by the Dvenixus for the purpose of observation and exploration, but nobody really knows where Hobart comes from.

Hobart has lived many lives enjoying the human planet earth, especially nature areas and miniature humans. At the end of each life cycle, Hobart mineralizes into a solid form but not before transferring biological components back to the mother mold to be recast with a brand new finish as a new Hobart.

Mineralized Hobart husks can be found in natural areas where Hobart goes to remold. Hobart has lived many lives as Hobart the Red, Hobart the Shiny, Hobart the Dissolvable, Hobart the Electrolytic, and so on. All of these husks may be visited, and sometimes are revisited by Hobart too.

Hobart loves rocks and photographs and visitors.
Tell Hobart a story.
Bring Hobart a rock.
